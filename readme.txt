== Description ==
Pixelo is a most minimalist ready theme for the Storyteller. A plain-vanilla & lightweight theme for a new blogger also clean and minimalist that allows your reader to focus on your content, build especially for daily or hobby bloggers who love to share their lifestyle, fashion, travel, entertainment, beauty or just a simple personal blog. Pixelo is a very fast, responsive, RTL & translation ready, best SEO practices. Pixelo theme best for single niche or multi niche blogging and SEO optimized.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= How to install Pixelo theme =
1. Login your WordPress dashboard.
2. Go to "Appearance" -> "Themes" and click on "Add New" button.
3. Click on "Upload Theme" button and choose the theme's zip file you have downloaded.
4. Click on "Install Now" button.
5. Activate the theme.
6. Now go to "Appearance" -> "Customize" to configure theme options.

Alternatively,

1. Login to your WordPress dashboard.
2. Go to "Appearance" -> "Themes" and click on "Add New" button.
3. Search for "Pixelo".
4. Click on Install and Activate.
