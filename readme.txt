Pixelo - Stepping into blogging. A Gutenberg-ready WordPress theme for Storytelling.

== Description ==
Pixelo is a most minimalist Gutenberg ready theme for Storytelling. A plain-vanilla & lightweight theme for a new blogger also clean and minimalist that allows your reader to focus on your content, build especially for daily or hobby bloggers who love to share their lifestyle, fashion, travel, entertainment, beauty or just a simple personal blog. Pixelo is a very fast, responsive and translation ready.
